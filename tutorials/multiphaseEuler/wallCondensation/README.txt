Example of water vapor condensation on a cooled wall.

Based on experimental data from the CONAN facility reported in:
Bucci, M., (2009).
Experimental and Computational Analysis of
Condensation Phenomena for the Thermalhydraulic Analysis of LWRs Containments.
PhD thesis, University of Pisa. https://hdl.handle.net/20.500.14242/132891

The tutorial corresponds to the experimental cases P10-T30-V15 to -V35.
